# Support Policy

Default support scope (recommended):

- Included:
  - Installation help
  - Setup guidance
  - Bug fixes for reported defects
- Not included:
  - Custom feature development
  - Third-party paid service costs
  - App store publishing on buyer accounts

Support channel:

- Email: your-support@email.com

Support hours:

- Sunday to Thursday
- Response target: within 24 to 48 hours

Before requesting support, buyer should provide:

1. App version and purchase code.
2. Device/OS details.
3. Error screenshots/logs.
4. Steps to reproduce the issue.
