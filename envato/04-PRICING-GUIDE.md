# Pricing Guide (Practical)

Recommended first listing price (CodeCanyon mobile source code):

- Starter range: USD 29 to USD 49
- Strong feature set + polished UI: USD 39 to USD 59

Suggested first launch:

- Start at USD 39
- Increase to USD 49 after first 5 to 10 sales and positive ratings

How revenue works:

- Envato adds buyer fee on top of your item price.
- Your net earning depends on exclusive/non-exclusive author fee rate.

Pricing strategy:

1. Start competitive to get first reviews.
2. Improve docs and demo quality.
3. Raise price gradually after traction.
